About Me

A little background for this blog.

My name is Summer Christiansen, and I am a graphic design major in my third year at BYU. This semester, I had the opportunity to accompany my husband on his study abroad in Jordan, where he was part of the intensive Arabic program.

Because I am a graphic design major, I dedicated much of my time in Jordan to learning Arabic calligraphy and typography, see more about my skills and experiences in my resume.

After three months in Amman, we started our two week long tour of Israel and the Holy Land. We visited many, many old ruins and archeological sites, as well as many churches dedicated to events in Jesus' life.
